The Problem

CREATORS CAN'T AFFORD THEIR SOUND

$20K+
Cost of a Home Studio

Professional studio equipment costs $20,000+. Independent creators — the fastest-growing segment — are locked out of studio quality by hardware cost alone.

$150/hr
Professional Studio Rate

Renting a professional studio runs $100–200/hr. For an indie artist recording an album, that's $3,000–10,000 just for tracking. Before mixing, mastering, or distribution.

100M+
Underserved Creators

Over 100 million independent artists and producers globally make music without access to professional production tools. The gap between bedroom and Billboard has never been wider.

100yr
Same Recording Tech

The fundamental architecture of music recording has been largely unchanged for a century. Spun Labs is the first AI-native approach to replacing the entire studio stack.

User Research · n=45 Creator Interviews

WHAT CREATORS TOLD US

75%
Rated their current sound quality ≤3/5

Three in four creators feel their recordings don't match their artistic vision. The gap between what they hear in their head and what they can capture is the core pain point.

100%
Expressed beta join intent

Every creator interviewed said they would try a product that could close the quality gap. Demand is not the problem — access, trust, and workflow integration are.

Key Pricing Insight

Research validated the $10–20/mo subscription target — anchored against current spend on plugins, DAW subscriptions, and occasional studio sessions. Value perception was high when quality promise was clear.

Go-To-Market Strategy

THE INDIE CREATOR WEDGE

01
Wedge Market

Target indie creators with 1K–100K followers — large enough to care about quality, small enough to be underserved by existing tools. High experimentation tolerance, vocal communities.

02
Community-Led Growth

Seed through producer communities, Discord servers, and music school networks. Creators trust peer recommendations over brand advertising. Let the sound do the selling.

03
Data Flywheel

Indie users → diverse audio training data → better models → expand to semi-pro → pro market. Each tier unlocks the next. Grassroots builds the moat.

04
Pro Expansion

Grammy-winner pilots validate quality at the top. 7 pilots already engaged. Top-down credibility + bottom-up adoption = defensible position across the full creator spectrum.

Positioning Framework I Developed

Spun Labs IS: AI tool for human-made music · Complete creative control · Bedroom to Billboard · Built for artists by artists.

Spun Labs IS NOT: A song generator · A fake-artist machine · Prompt-to-song · A replacement for musicians.

Reflection

WHAT I TOOK AWAY

"The best product insight doesn't come from the data —
it comes from listening to what creators are too embarrassed to say in a survey."

Working with Spun Labs taught me that user research in creative industries requires a different kind of listening. Creators don't articulate their pain as feature requests — they talk about their feelings. Frustration, inadequacy, comparison.

The $10–20/mo pricing insight came not from conjoint analysis but from a throwaway comment in interview 11: "I spend that on plugins I don't use anyway." That's the moment that mattered.
